Dantrolen prepared for malignant hyperthermia:
Malignant hyperthermia under general anesthesia rarely happens and paralyzes one’s body with severe fever. For that case, we always retain Dantrolen which is the only medicine to treat the symptom in a fast and safe way.
Bridion:
Wonjin provides Bridion, the medicine that only certified hospital can retain for patients who have difficulty breathing during the surgery. With Bridion, Wonjin patients are able to have their surgery in a safer operative environment.
